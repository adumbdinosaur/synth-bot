How Whitelist Words Work
  • Always Allowed: Messages that exactly match whitelist words are always allowed to be sent
  • Power Override: Even when power is 0, or sending would make power 0, these messages still go through
  • Exact Match: The entire message must match the whitelist word exactly (not just part of it)
  • Case Sensitivity: Choose whether matching should be case-sensitive or not
  • Emergency Use: Perfect for emergency words or important phrases that should never be blocked
