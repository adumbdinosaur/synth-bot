How Badwords Work
Detection & Filtering
  • Messages are scanned for badwords before sending
  • Detected badwords are replaced with <redacted>
  • Original message is deleted and replaced automatically
  • Case sensitivity can be configured per word
Energy Penalties
  • Each badword usage costs energy
  • Multiple badwords in one message add up
  • Higher penalties discourage usage
  • Energy recharges over time
Example: Message "This is damn stupid shit" with badwords "damn" (5 energy) and "stupid" (3 energy) becomes "This is <redacted> <redacted> shit" and costs 8 total energy.
